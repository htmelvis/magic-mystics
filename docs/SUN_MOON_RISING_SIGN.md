The Big Three overview
Suggested intro block (for the top of the screen):
Your birth chart is a snapshot of the sky at the exact moment you arrived, a map of light, shadow, and possibility.
Your Sun, Moon, and Rising — your “Big Three” — are the three main gateways into that map. The Sun shows how your light shines, the Moon reveals the tides of your inner world, and the Rising sign is the mask, doorway, and first impression you carry into the world.
(You can then have separate sections/cards for each.)
Sun sign
How your Sun sign is found
Your Sun sign is the zodiac sign the Sun was traveling through on the day you were born. It’s calculated using your date of birth, and only needs your birth time if the Sun happened to change signs that day.
Esoteric meaning of the Sun
The Sun is the steady flame at the center of your chart — the core of who you are when everything else falls away.
It speaks to your life force, ego, purpose, and creative will: the part of you that says “I am” and insists on becoming itself.
When you act from your Sun, you feel awake, decisive, and alive, as if the world is briefly orbiting around your essence. It is both your fuel and your trajectory, the story you came here to live on purpose.
Moon sign
How your Moon sign is found
Your Moon sign is the zodiac sign the Moon was in at the exact moment you were born.
Because the Moon moves quickly — changing signs every two to two and a half days — your Moon sign is calculated using your date, exact time, and place of birth to pinpoint its position in the sky.
Esoteric meaning of the Moon
The Moon is your inner tide — the hidden self that rises and falls beneath your public face.
It governs your emotions, instincts, and how you seek comfort and safety, the part of you that reacts before you can explain why.
Where your Sun describes how you shine in the open, your Moon describes how you feel in the dark: your private rituals, your emotional cravings, and the secret language of your heart.
When you honor your Moon sign, life feels softer, more aligned, as if your nervous system recognizes itself and can finally exhale.
Rising / Ascendant sign
How your Rising sign is found
Your Rising sign (Ascendant) is the zodiac sign that was literally rising over the eastern horizon at the moment you were born.
Astronomically, it’s calculated as the intersection of the eastern horizon with the zodiac at your birth time and location, and it shifts roughly every two hours — which is why you need an accurate birth time to find it.
Esoteric meaning of the Rising sign
Your Rising sign is your mask and doorway: the energy people sense before they truly know you.
It shows your style of approaching life, how you enter new spaces, and the aura you project without trying — the costume your soul chose for its first entrance on stage.
While your Sun is the inner script and your Moon is the whisper behind the curtain, your Rising sign is the opening scene: the way you introduce yourself to fate, and the stance your spirit takes as it meets the world.
Putting it together (optional block)
Short connective copy if you want a closing paragraph on the screen:
Together, your Sun, Moon, and Rising form a triad:
• The Sun: your core story and conscious will.
• The Moon: your emotional underworld and hidden tides.
• The Rising: your mask, aura, and first approach to life.
Explore each one, and you begin to see not just who you are — but how you came here to move through this lifetime.
